About Us

Welcome to Tyre Shopper, where we provide the easiest way to order new tyres online. Tyre Shopper is owned by Constant Price Monitor Ltd., which was established in 1994 with the goal of providing the widest selection of quality tyres at the lowest prices possible.

We’re proud to say that Tyre Shopper is the No.1 Online Tyre Retailer in the UK. We sell tyres over the internet and make bookings with our fitting partner, National, in one of their many branches that can fit the tyres to our customer’s vehicles (they also do mobile fittings).

Special Offers & Our Price Promise

We have special offers from popular tyre brands that present us with an opportunity to pass the savings onto our customers. Our Price Promise ensures that we beat our competitors’ prices by researching their prices of almost 400 tyres every week to make sure we remain cheaper. Our Price Promise is that should any new tyre that we offer are found by a customer to be less expensive elsewhere in the UK, we will drop our price to £1 below the competitor’s confirmed price.

FAQ & Tyre Information

Tyres are not always an easy subject to learn about. We have a frequently asked questions page which covers some of the most common questions about our business and the tyres too. There is also a tyre information page which is a good way to learn more about tyre technologies and how to pick a better tyre.

Many Ways to Search for Tyres

Our tyre search technology allows site visitors to search for new tyres using their own Vehicle Registration Number to find a suitable choice. It’s also possible to search by size, manufacturer and make & model.

Tyres Fittings Professionally Completed

Tyre fittings are conducted by one of more than 1,000 tyre technicians who have years of experience fitting tyres to many types of cars, 4x4’s and vans. The fitting centres are audited every quarter to ensure that the high standards are maintained.

Extensive Range

We have tens of thousands of tyre lines in stock at any given time for cars, 4x 4’s and vans. We have well-established relationships with all the major tyre brands as the largest online tyre seller in the UK. All prices quoted include VAT, delivery and fitting costs. Fitting includes the tyre, a new valve, a wheel balancing procedure, and a small environmental fee to dispose of the older tyre(s). All our tyres are brand new and fully guaranteed.

Every order placed comes with an email confirming the order, a detailed map to the local fitting centre, and turn-by-turn driving instructions for how to get there.

Our website is protected by Verisign 128-bit encryption technologies. Payment processing is completed by Streamline, the largest payment processor of online transactions in Europe.
